Chicken Stroganoff Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 1.5 lbs (700g) chicken breast, cubed.
  • Seasoning: Salt and black pepper to taste, 2 cloves of minced garlic.
  • Aromatics: 1 medium onion, finely chopped.
  • Fat: 2 tbsp butter or olive oil.

The Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (or 2 tbsp tomato paste).
  • 1 tbsp mustard (yellow or Dijon).
  • 1 tbsp ketchup.
  • 1 can (approx. 300g) Table Cream (Creme de Leite) or Heavy Cream.

    • Optional: 1 cup sliced mushrooms and "Batata Palha" (Shoestring potatoes) for serving.

    Instructions

    1. Season the Chicken: In a bowl, season the chicken cubes with salt, pepper, and minced garlic.
    2. Sear the Meat: Heat the butter or oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until golden brown. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.
    3. Sauté: In the same pan, add the chopped onion (and mushrooms if using). Sauté until the onion is translucent and soft.
    4. Build the Base: Return the chicken to the pan. Stir in the ketchup, mustard, and tomato sauce. Mix well for about 2 minutes to let the flavors meld.
    5. Finish with Cream: Lower the heat to low. Pour in the table cream (Creme de Leite). Stir gently until the sauce is uniform and hot, but do not let it boil, as the cream might separate.
    6. Serve: Taste and adjust the salt if necessary. Serve over white rice and top with a generous amount of shoestring potatoes for that essential crunch.

      7. Tip: If you prefer a darker sauce, you can add a splash of Worcestershire sauce or use a bit more tomato paste!
